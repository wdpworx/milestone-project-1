Welcome to Forever Perch

A Community Built for Perch Anglers

Welcome to Forever Perch—a community dedicated to anglers who share a passion for perch fishing. Whether you're exploring canals with ultralight tackle, working soft plastics along river margins, or searching deep lakes for specimen fish, Forever Perch is the place to share your catches, learn from others, and connect with anglers who appreciate the thrill of targeting one of the UK's most iconic freshwater predators. Every fish has a story. Every session teaches something new. Here, you can share both.

Become a Forever Perch Member

Membership unlocks everything you need to become part of the community.

Membership Includes

  • Access to our exclusive members-only forum
  • Monthly newsletters with tips, tricks, and stories from fellow anglers
  • Invitations to local meetups and events
  • Discounts on gear and tackle from our partners

Join us today and become part of a community that celebrates the art and excitement of perch fishing. Share your experiences, learn from others, and make lasting connections with fellow anglers.
